pie dough

hi, i’m sandy, a software engineer based in austin, tx.

i love pie, but making it is a struggle. you need space, patience, and skill. as a baker and a small-apartment-renter, i have none of these things.

but you know what? i love pie.

so i make it anyway, i mess it up, and i get better. as an engineer, i don’t mess up nearly as often (there’s a reason this isn’t an actual baking blog), but i do make mistakes.

i try things out, i experiment, i refactor, and i learn. this is a process i cherish, and every project i work on benefits from it.

i am an engineer who bakes her software. my ingredients are hand-selected, and my pie dough is light, flaky, and delicious.

“recipes” are projects and documentation i’ve written to learn new skills and build up my engineering cookbook.

“thoughts” are just that.
